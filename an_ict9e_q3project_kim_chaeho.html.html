Don't Panic The key factors that exacerbate the situation during natural catastrophes are panic and stress. People creating turmoil only serves to exacerbate disasters. It's critical to maintain composure during an earthquake. So that you don't act regrettably out of fear, you must keep your thoughts sensible.
Line Up On the way to the assembly area, the teacher should gently guide the children in a line. Locks on both doors must be removed.
Follow The Teachers To the designated gathering spot, teachers should Accompany their classes. BE PREPARED to select a different escape route in the event of a fire or blocked exits.
Pay attention to the directions Instructors should Direct pupils to evacuate upon taking a judgment that this is the most appropriate course of action.
